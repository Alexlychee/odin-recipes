Cedar Planked Salmon

Description

The salmon is moist, smoky, and by far the best I've ever eaten. I like to serve it with Asian-style rice and asparagus.

  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions
  • Fresh ginger
  • Minced garlic

Steps

  1. Soak the cedar planks.
  2. Make the marinade.
  3. Grill the planks until they start to smoke and crackle.
  4. Place the salmon on the planks, close the grill, and grill until the salmon is flaky.
